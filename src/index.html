What is it and why?

This is a simple little font containing 33 icons. There's one for each of the 32 Counties on the Island of Ireland, along with a glyph of the entire Island.

It's small, lightweight & scalable, which means it looks great on all different types of screens.

I created this for fun with Birdfont.
